We Offer High Quality Services

At Real Relaxed Dentistry, our aim is to make a positive difference in the lives of the youth and adults by offering the highest-quality dental care in a private-office atmosphere. By providing a team of professional, highly educated, honest, compassionate and committed employees, we aim to improve patient health, appearance, self-confidence and overall quality of life.

This philosophy is what Dr.Nxumalo firmly believes in and practices everyday, with each and every patient that walks through the door.

To Accomodate All Patients

Our Mission is to create a warm, friendly and comfortable dental environment which makes patients readily attend to their oral health whilst providing quality health care to ALL patients who enter our dental practice.

Help Our Customers Dental Education

Our Goal is to inform and educate patients about their oral health in an easily understandable and respectful manner; whilst simultaneously eliminating the stigma which perceives the dental setting as a cold and unpleasant environment where one is bound to experience pain or trauma.
